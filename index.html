About

Building at the Intersection of Healthcare & Technology

I'm Scott, an executive coach and strategic advisor based in Brooklyn, New York. I've spent my career focused on topics across healthcare, technology, and partnerships - from helping to scale partnerships at Headway while we grew 70x to investing in and advising executives at digital health companies such as Nourish, Onos, and Brellium . I also Chair the Growth Advisory Council for One Mind's Start Up Accelerator where I advise early stage behavioral health companies on their GTM strategies.

I am deeply curious, and get energy from working with executives to help them learn more about themselves, their values, and how they can better manage their teams and organizations.

I'm currently the GM of Care Partnerships at Headway, where I help health systems and medical groups connect patients to one of our 60k+ in-network behavioral health providers. In my free time, I love to bake, re-watch the Lord of the Rings, and run silly Dungeons and Dragons campaigns for friends.
